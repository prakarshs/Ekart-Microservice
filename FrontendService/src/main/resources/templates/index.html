Welcome To Our E-Kart ! This Is Our Home Page

This Is A Demo Spring Boot E-Commerce Microservice Project. Welcome to eKart's cutting-edge microservices-powered platform! Our platform comprises three integral services: StockService, OrderService, and PaymentService, each seamlessly orchestrated to provide you with an exceptional shopping experience. The StockService ensures products are readily available, the OrderService manages efficient processing, and the PaymentService guarantees secure transactions. With Spring Boot as our foundation and MySQL databases ensuring data integrity, eKart delivers reliability and innovation right to your fingertips. Start exploring, shopping, and enjoying the future of e-commerce with us!
